Admissions 2026–27

Join Our School Family

Admissions are open for Std 5 to 10 for the academic year 2026–2027. Free uniform, shoes, and books provided to all students.

1

Collect Form

Visit the school office at Borda, Margao or call us to get the admission form.

2

Submit Documents

Submit form with birth certificate, previous school report card, and passport photo.

3

Verification

Documents will be verified by administration within 2–3 working days.

4

Enroll & Start!

Receive your confirmation, free uniform, shoes, and books. Welcome to PM SHRI!

🏅 NSQF Certified School — Retail & IT Vocational Courses

We offer Industry-Aligned Career Subjects under NSQF (National Skills Qualifications Framework) in Retail and IT — giving our students a head start in the job market right from school.
